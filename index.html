Rules

Work in progress...

Please contact me if you think something should be added, changed, or removed.

Griefing

You Can:

  • Prank others, so long as they can get their stuff back & their base isn't damaged

You cannot:

  • Steal
  • Suffocate
  • Portal Trap

Conduct

Language:

  • Slurs are not allowed
  • Feel free to kill others, but save their stuff and don't target an individual constantly

Machines

There aren't any hard rules for machines, It's more a guideline.

Guidelines:

  • If a machine runs every few ticks, please add a toggle
  • Don't create purposeful lag machines
  • Allowed Dupes: Carpet, String, Tripwire Hooks, Coral, Falling blocks
  • No other dupes are allowed
